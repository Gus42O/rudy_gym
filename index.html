🦵 ACL-SPECIFIC PROTOCOL
⚠ Red Flags — STOP Training: Sharp knee pain · Swelling/effusion · Giving way sensation · Pain above 3/10 during any exercise
Test all movements at 50% intensity before starting full sets. If pain-free at 50%, proceed. If not, regress or skip.
PROGRESSION RULES — WEEK BY WEEK
  • →
    Week 1 Foundation — Isometrics & Machines
    Isometric quad holds (4×20 sec), lying hamstring curls, leg press (90° max), calf raises, glute bridges. Zero dynamic unilateral loading. Pain-free range only. All HIIT is seated/upper-body. Fasted cardio = light stationary bike or slow walk at Zone 1 (able to hold full conversation).
  • →
    Week 2 Foundation Build — Progressing Loads
    Slight weight increase on Leg Press and Hamstring Curls vs Week 1. Introduce adductor machine. Maintain machine-only lower body. Begin tracking pain response after each session — aim for 0/10 next morning. Fasted cardio: same Zone 1, extend to 35 mins if comfortable.
  • →
    Week 3 Strength Build — Introduce Split Squat
    Introduce front-foot elevated split squat (RPE 6–7/10). Single-plane movements only. Increase RDL to moderate load. HIIT upgrades to moderate circuit (step-ups allowed if pain-free). Fasted cardio: 30 min Zone 1 stationary bike or brisk walk. Posture sequence to be done 6x/week.
  • →
    Week 4 Strength Build — Progressive Overload
    Heavier split squats, RDL, and leg press (RPE 8/10). Rotational core work intensifies — increase cable resistance. Moderate HIIT continues. Fasted cardio: 30 min Zone 1 target HR 100–120 bpm. Optional jogging ONLY if cleared by PT and pain-free on all walking tests.
  • →
    Week 5 Peak Intensity — Max Effort
    Heaviest week across all lifts (RPE 8–9/10). Explosive woodchops and rotational power introduced. High-intensity HIIT (4 rounds). Fasted cardio: 30 min Zone 1 (can include light jog intervals if PT-cleared — 30 sec jog / 90 sec walk). No pivoting, single-leg jumping, or uncontrolled deceleration without PT clearance.
  • ⛔
    Never Until Cleared PT Clearance Required
    Single-leg jumping · Pivot/cutting movements · Uncontrolled deceleration · High-impact landing · Running above Zone 2 HR without PT sign-off.
KEY STABILIZERS — EVERY SESSION
  • →
    VMO Activation 3×12 daily
    Leg extensions with toes slightly inward. Crucial for ACL health and patellar tracking.
  • →
    Hamstring Curls 3×10 every session
    ACL synergist — hamstring strength directly protects the graft from anterior shear.
  • →
    Hip Abductor Raises 3×15 daily
    Sidelying leg lifts. Controls valgus collapse (knee caving inward) during all loaded movements.
  • →
    Glute Bridges 3×15 daily
    Hip extension strength and posterior chain activation. Reduces anterior knee forces.
🧍 POSTURE & ROTATION CORRECTION
🗓 Daily Mobility Sequence: 10 mins · 6x/week. Perform in the morning or before your workout. Non-negotiable for thoracic and spinal health.
